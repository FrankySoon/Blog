Anant Shrankhla

Anant Shrankhla is an indie game project developed by InstanityCrew, a team led by AsasinoManik. AsasinoManik is a video game designer and YouTuber with over 18.5k subscribers. He began his game development career in 10th grade while working at his father’s café, where he fell in love with creating games. After working on several projects, he decided to develop his own indie game.

The project, initially known as "ABE CHUP: A Mysterious Journey," was later renamed Anant Shrankhla. While he started the project alone, he eventually gathered a team of passionate individuals via Discord to help him achieve his dream. On January 7, 2021, the first glimpses of the game were revealed through an alpha demo game trailer. See the video.

About the Game

This first-person mystery adventure game is deeply rooted in Indian culture. It follows a clueless protagonist who finds himself in an unknown place in the Himalayas. The story revolves around mysterious voices, places, and narratives. The game is a heart-pumping adventure through historical Indian landmarks and their mysteries, ultimately leading the protagonist to discover his true purpose.
